Pineapple Pepperoni Pasta
images/Pineapple Pepperoni Pasta.jpg
Dinner
Pineapple, cheese, oreos, pasta, pepperoni.

1. Cook your desired amount of pasta according to the instructions on the package
2. Dice the pepperoni into small slices
3. Slice the pineapple and cheese into cubes
4. In a large pan heat a small amount of olive oil at medium heat
5. Turn the heat to low and add the pepperoni slices to cook and become slightly crispy
6. Add the pineapple cubes and cook until slightly softened
7. Add the cheese and the cooked pasta to the pan and mix everything together
8. Serve in a bowl and crush oreos on top
