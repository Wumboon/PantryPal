Cheesy Salsa Pasta Bites
images/Cheesy Salsa Pasta Bites.jpg
Dinner
Pasta, Cheese, Salsa, Pepperonis. Oreos.

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Prepare a baking dish with non-stick or olive oil spray
3. In a pot over medium-high heat cook pasta according to package instructions. Once cooked al dente, drain and set aside
4. In a skillet over medium heat, melt ¼ cup of shredded cheese and add salsa
5. Once cheese is fully melted, mix in cooked pasta, stir, and remove from heat
6. Layer pepperoni slices in the prepped baking dish and spoon the pasta mixture on top
7. Top with remaining ¾ cup of shredded cheese and oreos
8. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes or until cheese is fully melted
9. Serve warm and enjoy!
