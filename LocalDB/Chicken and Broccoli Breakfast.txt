Chicken and Broccoli Breakfast
Breakfast
chicken, onion, broccoli
 
1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add a tablespoon of oil. 
2. Once the oil is hot, add the diced onion and cook until translucent. 
3. Add the diced chicken and cook until it is lightly browned. 
4. Add the chopped broccoli and stir-fry for a few minutes. 
5. Once the vegetables are almost cooked, remove the skillet from the heat and serve.
